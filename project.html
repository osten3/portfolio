What do we do at the Dayton Regional STEM School?

What is PBL?

PBL is something we call project base learning. We use this so we can learn more in a productive way at school. As in projects we work most of the time in groups to complete a project. It saves time and we learn more by getting it done. PBL is something that we used up until 12th grade.

What is Genius Hour?

Genius Hour is a project of where we research a question we have and find information on it. Since I am big in music I created a page explaining my theory of music.
