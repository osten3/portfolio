R.A.C.E. Club

As a Senior I am currently a board member of the R.A.C.E. club we have at our school. The Racial Alliance Collaboration Effort is an amazing club that educated peers and teachers about race and ethnicity. So far this year, we have planned to collaborate with the whole school and ask them what there thoughts are about race and culture through our school. Leading up to how they feel will give us more information on what to do in the following months. We also have planned to talk about black history month, and women's rights month.
