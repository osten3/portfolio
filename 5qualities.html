The 5 Qualities at DRSS

We Have 5 qualities in our school to help us better learn and get us doing what our school is known for. This includes PLB and coding which has created this website. The 5 qualities are Creativity, Collaboration, Communication, Inquiry, and Persistence. I will explain what these mean below and how we use them.

Creativity

The use of the imagination or original ideas, especially in the production of an artistic work.

Collaboration

The action of working with someone to produce or create something.

Communication

The imparting or exchanging of information or news.

Inquiry

An act of asking for information.

Persistence

Firm or obstinate continuance in a course of action in spite of difficulty or opposition.
